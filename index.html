About Us
Krity 360: Your trusted guide to aesthetics, tailored just for you.
Welcome to Krity 360, an aesthetic clinic.
Dr. Kavita Raghotham is a distinguished Maxillofacial Surgeon with extensive expertise in facial aesthetic surgery and a profound passion in the field of facial aesthetics. She graduated from JSS Medical University and completed her post-graduation in Maxillofacial Surgery from Mangalore. In addition, she received specialized training and fellowship in cleft &craniofacial surgery, as well as numerous surgical observerships at Tata Memorial Hospital, Mumbai and RCC, Trivandrum.
Dr. Raghotham is also the founder of Krity Wellness located near Sarjapur Road in Bengaluru that offers an array of services such as medi facials, chemical peels, microneedling, lasers, Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP) for skin and hair rejuvenation treatment options along with anti-aging treatments.
The clinic also provide treatment for wrinkles and fillers to enhance your appearance. The clinic even has facilities dedicated to aesthetic dentistry while offering hair transplant surgeries too!
